Rules and Guidelines
Unstop Registration
Startups already registered on platforms like Unstop do not need to register again on the website.
Verification
Teams will be required to provide proof for the aforementioned aspects at a later time. Failure to do so will result in disqualification.
Team Member
The core team should consist of no more than 5 members, who can be college students, professors, or working professionals.
Single Track per Idea
You can participate in multiple tracks with different business ideas, but each idea must be registered in only one zone and one track.
Team Leader
Only the team leader needs to register on the portal. They can add their team members later.
Eligibility
Startups with investments valuing 5 crores or more are not eligible to participate.
